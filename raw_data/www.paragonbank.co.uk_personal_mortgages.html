Working with us

You'll need to speak with an Independent Financial Adviser to apply for your first buy-to-let mortgage with us, but once you're on board, you can easily manage your existing mortgages, and apply for new ones, with us online.

When the time comes, we make it easy to switch products, apply for extra funds to improve your property or expand your portfolio, and get support for making your home more energy efficient.

We understand that every landlord's situation is unique. From single properties to HMOs and multi-unit blocks, we've built our services around real landlord needs.

You'll find everything you need to know about our current mortgages and services right here, along with helpful resources to support your buy-to-let journey.

  1. Find an adviser or intermediary
  2. Apply for a buy-to-let mortgage
  3. Once approved, register and log in to your online account
  4. Manage your account or apply for additional products

The National Residential Landlords Association (NRLA). The UK's largest membership organisation for private residential landlords.

Propertymark is a membership body for property agents. Find an estate or letting agent that is Propertymark Protected​.

The UK’s leading landlord website, providing property news, advice, legal information and insight for the rental property industry.

The Law Society is the professional body for solicitors in England and Wales. Their find a Solicitor service helps you to find an official legal professional.​
